Zooki

Group Project for Game Design course at William & Mary

  • Summary: Zooki the penguin's favorite food is ice cream, but all the ice cream is melting because of global warming. In order to survive, he needs to store as much ice cream as possible where it will be safe and cold in his igloo.
  • Goal: Run, jump, and slide to navigate the maze-like terrain and collect enough ice cream cones before they melt. But watch out - the floor is lava!

Responsibilities

  • Character and background story development; named Zooki
  • Created sprite sheets, graphics, concept art, and sound effects
  • Utilized Visual Studio for level design
  • Wrote design document, made slides for project presentation
