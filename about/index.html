I'm a right-brained, extroverted computer scientist with a passion for art.

I'm Elizabeth Dabbs, a senior consultant for Booz Allen Hamilton working from West Palm Beach, FL. As a developer for the Digital Interactive team, I specialize in integrating client-provided content into responsive, dynamic websites. I enjoy projects that incorporate creativity and challenge me to learn new technologies.

I'm a Richmond, Virginia native and have been making art for as long as I can remember. It wasn't until college that I discovered my love for coding, which led me to explore the intersection of design, problem solving, and technology.
