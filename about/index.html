Hi, I'm Lizzie.

I love to draw connections between the seemingly unrelated. The theme of my blog is a quote from Leonardo daVinci: “Study the science of art. Study the art of science. Develop your senses- especially learn how to see. Realize that everything connects to everything else.” Follow along on my renaissance journey, as I seek to make connections and learn to see.

I currently live in Princeton, New Jersey and work for Booz Allen Hamilton, specializing in front-end web development for their Strategic Innovation Group. I'm a Richmond, Virginia native and have been making art for as long as I can remember. It wasn't until college that I discovered my love for coding, which led me to explore the intersection of design, problem solving, and technology.
