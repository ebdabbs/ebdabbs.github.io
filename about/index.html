Bio

I'm Elizabeth Dabbs, a consultant for Booz Allen Hamilton in the Washington, D.C. area. As a front-end developer for the Digital Interactive team, I specialize in integrating client-provided content into responsive, dynamic websites. I enjoy projects that incorporate creativity and challenge me to learn new technologies.

I'm a Richmond, Virginia native and have been making art for as long as I can remember. It wasn't until college that I discovered my love for coding, which lead me to explore the intersection of design, logic, and technology.
